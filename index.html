What is a TekPocket?

TekPockets are stylish quilted neck pouches that keeps everything easily accessible all in one place! No more juggling keys, phone, ID cards, etc. Display convention badges, name tags, work badges, business cards and more in the clear vinyl pocket.

Keep boarding passes and luggage tickets safe while traveling. There is even a pouch for headphones for hands-free phone use while on the go and your phone is right at your fingertips.

TekPocket can hold an iPhone 6s comfortably, measuring 7.5" long X 6" wide.
